You have questions

We have answers.

Who gets into CPOHQ?

CPOHQ is apply-only and vetted. Membership is reserved for sitting Chief People Officers and senior people leaders at companies that meet our bar.

What is the AI Chief of Staff?

An always-on agent that reads your governed people data and answers the questions you'd otherwise wait weeks for — every number traced to its source.

How long until the agents are live?

About a month, and roughly two hours of your HRIS owner's time to connect sources. We handle reconciliation and governance from there.

Is my people data safe?

Yes. It's never used to train models or shared with third parties. SOC 2 Type 2 and GDPR compliant, encrypted in transit and at rest.

Do I have to use both the community and the agents?

No. Join the community, deploy the agents, or do both. Most leaders end up doing both, but you can start with either.
